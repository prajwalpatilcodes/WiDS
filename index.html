Women in Data Science (WiDS) Datathon is a global initiative led by Stanford University, which aims to inspire women worldwide to learn more about data science. The WiDS Datathon encourages women worldwide to hone their data science skills, creating a supportive environment for women to connect with others in their community who share their interests. Data scientists of all levels are invited to participate in the datathon, including beginners.

The 6th Annual WiDS Datathon 2023 Challenge is on “Adapting to Climate Change by Improving Extreme Weather Forecasts”.

KLE Technological University, (WiDS Hubballi) is hosting a workshop in support of the global Datathon challenge to assist the participants to hone the required data science skillsets and participate effectively. Data science experts will engage in the sessions during this workshop and provide insights into analytical solutions for real world problems.

Debanjana Banerjee
Senior Data Scientist, Walmart Global Tech
Topic
AIR: Automated Item Recommendation
for Online Campaigns
She is a Data Scientist at Walmart Labs since 2017 with her research focused heavily on Rare Events, NLP and Stochastic Processes. She has worked on Anomaly Detection, Biclustering, Recommender Systems and most recently, Computer Vision and Intervention Analysis.

She is strongly driven by the will to innovate and push the boundaries of the state-of-the-art. Prior to this, She has completed her MS in Mathematical Statistics and Machine Learning from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur.

Topic

She is a Senior Data and Applied Scientist at Microsoft and had various research roles of IBM.
